3 Quick Tips to Declutter Your Email Inbox

- Use the search bar to find and unsubscribe from newsletters you don’t read.
- Create folders for categories like “Bills,” “Family,” and “Work.”
- Archive old emails instead of deleting — you can still search them later.

How to Back Up Your Photos Safely

Use a combination of cloud storage (Google Photos, iCloud) and an external hard drive for maximum security.

Make Your Phone Easier to Use

Adjust text size, turn on “Do Not Disturb” at night, and learn a few quick gestures to save time.
